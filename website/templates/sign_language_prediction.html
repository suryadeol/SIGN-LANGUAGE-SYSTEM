Sign Language Gesture Information

Here, you can find information about the sign language gestures. Each gesture corresponds to a specific letter or action:

Class Description
A to Z Alphabets from A to Z in sign language.
Del Symbol for deleting a letter or element.
Nothing Label that will be printed when nothing is given.
Space Used to represent a space in sign language.

User Interaction Section

Upload an image, and we will predict the sign language gesture it represents. Supported gestures include alphabets A to Z, 'Delete', 'Nothing', and 'Space'. Simply choose an image file and click the 'Predict' button to get the result.
