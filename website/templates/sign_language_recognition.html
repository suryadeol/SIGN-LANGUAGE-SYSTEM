Sign Language Gesture Information

Here, you can find information about the sign language gestures. Each gesture corresponds to a specific letter or action:

Category Description

Deleting Gesture indicating the deletion of a letter or element.
Spacing Gesture representing a space in sign language.
Nothing Label for when no specific gesture is detected.
